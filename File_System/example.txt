Geography and demographics
Location: India is a peninsula located in South Asia, bordered by the Indian Ocean, the Arabian Sea, and the Bay of Bengal.
Area: It is the seventh-largest country in the world by land area.
Population: As the most populous country, it has a population of nearly 1.4 billion people.
Capital: The capital of India is New Delhi.
Landscape: India's landscape varies dramatically from the snow-capped Himalayas in the north to the tropical rain forests in the south.
